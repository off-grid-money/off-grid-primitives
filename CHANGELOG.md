## [0.1.0] - 2025-01-30

### Added

- Spot primitives: L1/L2/L3, `Pair`, `OrderBook`, `Order`, `MatchingEngine`, `TimeInForce`, events.
- Account primitives: `AccountBalances`, `collect_balances`, spot/futures/option account modules.
- Asset module structure: spot, futures, option submodules (placeholders).
